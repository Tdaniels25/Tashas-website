the classic menu

As much as I love cooking, I don’t enjoy complicated recipes and fussy food. I value simple, beautiful food.
I want the ingredients to stand out. At tashas our food is comforting, unpretentious and honest. It’s also memorable – because we constantly strive to innovate and fascinate.
Every dish that is brought to your table epitomises our philosophy: consistent in authenticity and quality, but with a unique twist, because each dish is bespoke and made on order.

Everything on our Classic menu is based on timeless café classics that we love and know so well When you arrive at tashas, you will receive both a Classic menu and an Inspired By menu. Classic menu is offered at all of our locations and is filled with decadent, comfort food. Quality and generosity are our watchwords – we never cut corners or portions. We use the freshest, best ingredients from the finest suppliers. We aim to change the Classic menu once a year. I hate taking an item off the menu – I worry about the one customer who misses something specific that isn’t available any more. The good news is that when we take a dish off the menu, we can put something new on!
